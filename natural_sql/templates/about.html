About me

I’m a master’s student in Data Science at Uppsala University, with a focus on Machine Learning Engineering. While I don’t focus on any particular area within data science, most of my projects revolve around deep learning applications.
During my bachelor’s in Mathematics at Institut Teknologi Bandung, I explored time-series deep learning models for stock price prediction as part of my thesis.
Currently, for my master’s thesis, I am working on computer vision for physics. Specifically, I’m researching how segmentation networks, combined with spatial weighting mechanisms and synthetic data, can be used to segment cross-sectional images of superconducting wires based on material type.
